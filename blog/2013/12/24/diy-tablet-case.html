So I bought a China tablet (Colorfly E708 Q1) for my mom this holiday.

But I didn't get the chance to buy her a tablet case. My mom wants one, but the malls are so crowded. I hate crowded places so I decide to buy her one after the holidays. So why not play a little joke in the mean time? I decided to make her a case out of illustration boards and folders. I love doing these home made crafts during high school. I would make my own action figures during my free time (recess and weekends). Here are some of those which survived through the years.

Anyway, here's what I've done:

measurements on illustration board
measurements on folder
applied folder to tablet
slightly cut and folded the illustration board
hot glue applied
magic!

This will serve as her case in the mean time. Anyway, Merry Christmas!!!
